Frequently Asked Questions

  • Order Tracking FAQ

    Track your order by entering your order number and email address on our tracking page. Orders typically process within 1-2 business days and shipping takes 3-7 days depending on your location.

    International orders may take 10-20 business days to arrive. You'll receive tracking information via email once your order ships.

  • Money Back Guarantee

    We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all our products. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, return it within 30 days for a full refund.

    Items must be unused and in their original packaging. Please note that shipping costs are non-refundable.

  • How do I cancel my order?

    You can cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it by logging into your account and visiting the "Order History" section.

    For orders that have already begun processing, please contact our customer service team at support@fureverfriends.com or use the chat feature for assistance.

  • Why is my order cancelled due to failed delivery?

    Orders can be cancelled due to failed delivery for several reasons:

    • Incorrect or incomplete shipping address
    • No one available to receive the package after multiple delivery attempts
    • Package was refused at delivery
    • Shipping carrier couldn't access the delivery location

    If your order was cancelled for this reason, please contact us to arrange redelivery or refund.
